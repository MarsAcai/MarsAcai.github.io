I'm a Pop Artist. This means that if I was poked by a pin, I'd loudly go *POP!* as all the hot air inside went rushing out. Being a pop artist comes with a certain levity- I am very light- but that's sometimes replaced by a sense of unease. When going about my business in the outside world, I have to be careful to avoid anything sharp that could snag on me.

Other pop artists have been popped by touching the thorns on rose bushes, bumping into the sharp corners of buildings, and getting shot by critical members of the public. Many pop artists try to 'limit their exposure' to the harmful things in the world around them, but it's questionable if that's really much of an answer. Pop artists who keep themselves completely safe experience the sensation of deflating as the air slowly leaks out of them anyway.
